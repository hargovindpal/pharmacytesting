ADMISSION

Eligibility Criteria for B.Pharm (04 Years)

Candidate must have passed 10+2 examination conducted by the respective state/central government authorities with English as one of the subjects and Physics and Chemistry as compulsory subjects along with Mathematics /Biology with at least 50% marks in the above subjects, taken together and 50% marks overall.

Relaxation – 05% relaxation in the minimum eligibility is provided to SC/ST Category Students.
Minimum Age – Candidate must attain the age of 17 years as on (31.12.2024)

Eligibility Criteria for B.Pharm (Lateral Entry-03 Years)

Candidate must have passed Diploma in Pharmacy from a PCI approved Institution, with at least 60% marks.
Relaxation–05% relaxation in the minimum eligibility is provided to SC/ST Category Students.
Minimum Age–Candidate must attain the age of 18 years as on (31.12.2024)

Eligibility Criteriafor D.Pharm(02 Years)

Candidate must have passed 10+2 examination with PCB / PCM with at least 50% marks by the respective state/central government authorities.
Relaxation–05%relaxation in the minimum eligibility is provided to SC/ST Category Students.
Minimum Age–Candidate must attain the age of 17 years as on (31.12.2024)
Selection Mode – Candidates must have to appear for the state level entrance examination JEECUP, which is required for admission to diploma courses in Uttar Pradesh including Diploma in Pharmacy.
